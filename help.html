Rsampling is a free and open source package intended for teaching and learning about resampling in statistics. It is heavily based on the Resampling Stats.

We may summarize the logic behind a significance test as follows:

  • Determine your statistic of interest
  • Determine your null hypothesis
  • Obtain the distribution of your statistic of interest under the null hypothesis
  • Reject the null hypothesis if the probability of the observed statistic of interest under the null hypothesis is under a certain threshold

The idea behind this package is to help students to understand the logic behind these tests, with lots of flexibility on what is the statistic that will be calculated.

Rsampling and and this web interface were written by Andre Chalom, Alexandre Adalardo and Paulo Prado.
